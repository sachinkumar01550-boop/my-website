About Sai Meer Technical University

Our History

Established in 1998, Sai Meer Technical University has been at the forefront of technical education for over two decades. We have grown from a small engineering college to a full-fledged technical university with over 10,000 students.

Our alumni have gone on to work at leading tech companies around the world, contributing to innovation and technological advancement.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a globally recognized center of excellence in technical education and research.

Mission: To provide quality education that transforms students into skilled professionals, innovators, and leaders who can contribute to society.
